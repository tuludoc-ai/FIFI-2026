What is FIFI?

LLMs can rewrite scientific titles into three styles — Technical, Accessible, and Catchy. FIFI challenges participants to reverse that transformation: given only a reformulated title, retrieve or reconstruct the original research title.

🔍

Subtask 1: Find It

Rank up to 10 candidate titles by likelihood of being the true source. Evaluated with MRR@10.

✍️

Subtask 2: Fix It

Reconstruct the original title from its stylistic variant. Evaluated with F1 Score & BERTScore.
